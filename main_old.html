Take your team to the extreme with customized music, choreography and training from Extreme Athletics, led by Sam Owens, head coach of the University of Minnesota Spirit Squad.

Extreme Athletics is founded on the principle of building great kids through the sport of cheerleading. Coach Owens started in 1999, providing athletes and coaches with customized content and professional instruction designed specifically for the unique needs of their teams. Good sportsmanship, safety, personal development and healthy competition are the goals for every Extreme Team!

Routines are carefully choreographed for each team and athletes are thoroughly instructed by experienced and professionally trained staff. Each routine is designed to challenge athletes while staying within the skill level of the team. Instruction is provided using proper techniques and without compromising safety.

Be Your Best!
Extreme Athletics provides physical, emotional and intellectual coaching to young athletes through cheerleading, dance and music. Its coaches focus on developing youth into emotionally and physically strong adults by helping each athlete reach his/her highest potential. Athletes earn self esteem and pride from performing intense, disciplined routines that showcase their talent and skills. Responsibility, a sense of belonging and sportsmanship are felt by working with other athletes and competing against other teams.

Feel the Success!
With successful training comes a sense of pride and accomplishment that lead to positive attitudes and life changing outcomes. Being part of a team teaches healthy interdependence that will benefit athletes as young adults and stay with them for the rest of their lives. Self-discipline, self-worth and personal responsibility learned through our programs establish a healthy foundation for athletes to utilize in all aspects of their social and professional futures.

OUR MISSION
TESTIMONIALS
Residential Camps
Launch each season with an Extreme Athletics cheer camp! Summer cheer camp provides your team with a jump start to your fall practice. Accelerate your team’s skill level before your season starts by capitalizing on what they learn during the summer. Our all-inclusive camps offer sessions to refresh your team’s basic skills and introduce them to new and challenging material.

College Prep Camp
Prepare for college tryouts. This camp is designed for individual athletes who intend on cheering at the college level. The camp will strengthen skills that are needed for college tryouts, such as co-ed and all-girl stunting, pyramids, jumps, motions and tumbling.

Choreography
Blow away the competition with an Extreme routine personally choreographed by Coach Owens. You will receive the most innovative content with amazing choreography guaranteed to get your team noticed. Routines from Extreme Athletics consistently finish among the top in the nation and your team can be part of the action.

Customized Music
An Extreme routine includes exciting and original music, digitally mastered for your choreography. Include your team’s name or mascot in the music to personalize it even more! Our music will have your fans on their feet during your performance.

Private Camp
Hosted at a location of your choice; training can range from a few hours to as long as a week. Instruction includes clinics for Basic Skills, Transitions and Choreography.
